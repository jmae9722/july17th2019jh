Best Sneakers of All Time

  1. Jordan 1's

  2. VaporMax

  3. Yves Saint Laurent Opyum Sandals

  4. Giuseppe Zanotti Snake Strap Sandals

  5. Air Force 1's

  1. Jordan 1's, specifically the breds, have a history in the NBA and in the creator, Michael Jordan's career. They have a nickname called the 'banned' shoe. They apparently were banned because of the colorway. There was too much black.
  2. The VaporMax is a very aesthetically pleasing air max. It's one of the most expensive air max's if not the most expensive.
  3. Yves Saint Laurent's Opyum sandals are another aesthetically beautiful pair of shoes with their recognizable moniker as the heel. This luxury shoe retails to at least 2,000.
  4. Giuseppe Zanotti's snake strap sandals are another pretty pair of heels. The 'snake' as the strap of the sandal adds an even more elegant vibe to the pump.
  5. The very classic Air Force ones are a cultural staple everywhere. From being mentioned in rap songs to even acquiring nicknames like 'G Fazo' in certain communties, it's impact is undeniable.
